# Refresh: What's this about?

Highlights of the Jupyter AI workshop in Seattle

What we did there:

<v-clicks>

- Previewed the *multi-agent, multi-chat, notebook-native experience* in Jupyter AI v3.0 beta
- Presented our *new real-time collaboration (RTC) system* in `jupyter_server_documents` (JSD)
- Previewed a new project: `nb-cli`
- Explained how Jupyter AI works
- Helped users discover how they could use Jupyter AI to do useful things
- Shared ideas about the future direction of Jupyter AI

</v-clicks>
